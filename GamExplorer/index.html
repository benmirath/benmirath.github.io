GamExplorer

At a loss for what game to play next? Try out this tool by inputting different games below to gain a new perspective on your tastes! As you add a new game, it is compared to all previous entries, by one of several factors. These so far include the gameplay genre, story themes, or mechnaical concepts featured in each game.
